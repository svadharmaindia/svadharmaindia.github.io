About Svadharma India

Rooted in tradition  ·  Offered with devotion

"Every soul carries within it the map of its own dharma. Jyotiṣa illuminates that map — not to bind us to fate, but to help us walk our path with awareness and grace."

Svadharma India is rooted in the living tradition of Vedic astrology and Indian philosophical knowledge. Each consultation and class is offered with sincerity, depth, and devotion — drawing from classical texts, personalised observation, and a genuine love for these ancient systems.

Whether you seek clarity on life's direction through Jyotiṣa, or wish to introduce your child to the wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita in a way that feels alive and modern, you are warmly welcome here.
